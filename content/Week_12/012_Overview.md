# Course Structure

## Module 5: Deep Learning Foundations (Weeks 10-12)
- Focus: Core concepts and architectures in deep learning.
- Topics include neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, image and sequence processing.

### Week 12: Recurrent Neural Networks and Sequence Processing
- **Lesson 56:** Introduction to Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs)
  - Overview of RNNs and their importance in sequence modeling.
  - Math Focus: Sequential data processing and hidden state propagation.

- **Lesson 57:** Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) Networks
  - Understanding and implementing LSTM architecture.
  - Math Focus: Gates mechanism and cell state management.

- **Lesson 58:** Gated Recurrent Units (GRU) and Advanced RNN Architectures
  - Learning GRU and bidirectional RNNs.
  - Math Focus: GRU gates and bidirectional processing.

- **Lesson 59:** Text Processing and Natural Language Processing with RNNs
  - Implementing text generation and sequence classification.
  - Math Focus: Word embeddings and sequence-to-sequence models.

- **Lesson 60:** Time Series Forecasting with RNNs
  - Applying RNNs to time series prediction problems.
  - Math Focus: Temporal dependencies and forecasting techniques.
