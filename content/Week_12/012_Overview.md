  # Course Structure

 ## Module 5: Deep Learning Foundations (Weeks 10-12)
- Focus: Core concepts and architectures in deep learning.
- Topics include neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, image and sequence processing.

### Week 12: Recurrent Neural Networks and Sequence Processing
- **Lesson 56:** Introduction to Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs)
  - Overview of RNNs and their applications in sequence modeling.
  - Math Focus: Recurrent connections and temporal dependencies.

- **Lesson 57:** Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) Networks
  - Understanding LSTM architecture and its advantages over vanilla RNNs.
  - Math Focus: LSTM cell structure, gates, and gradient flow.

- **Lesson 58:** Gated Recurrent Units (GRU) and Advanced RNN Architectures
  - Implementing GRU networks and bidirectional RNNs.
  - Math Focus: GRU simplification and bidirectional processing.

- **Lesson 59:** Sequence-to-Sequence Models and Applications
  - Building seq2seq models for machine translation and text generation.
  - Math Focus: Encoder-decoder architecture and attention mechanisms.

- **Lesson 60:** Time Series Prediction with Deep Learning
  - Applying RNNs and LSTMs to time series forecasting.
  - Math Focus: Temporal patterns and prediction horizons.
