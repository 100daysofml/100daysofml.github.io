  # Course Structure

 ## Module 5: Deep Learning Foundations (Weeks 10-12)
- Focus: Core concepts and architectures in deep learning.
- Topics: Neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, image and sequence processing.

### Week 10: Introduction to Deep Learning and CNNs
- **Lesson 46:** Introduction to Neural Networks and Deep Learning
  - Overview of neural networks and deep learning fundamentals.
  - Math Focus: Activation functions and forward propagation.

- **Lesson 47:** Building Neural Networks with TensorFlow and Keras
  - Understanding and implementing neural networks using modern frameworks.
  - Math Focus: Backpropagation and gradient descent optimization.

- **Lesson 48:** Introduction to Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
  - Learning the fundamentals of CNNs and their architecture.
  - Math Focus: Convolution operations and feature maps.

- **Lesson 49:** Implementing CNNs for Image Classification
  - Building and training CNNs for real-world image classification tasks.
  - Math Focus: Pooling operations, dropout, and regularization in CNNs.

- **Lesson 50:** Transfer Learning and Pre-trained Models
  - Exploring transfer learning and using pre-trained CNN models.
  - Math Focus: Fine-tuning and feature extraction techniques.
