# Course Structure

## Module 5: Deep Learning Foundations (Weeks 10-12)
- Focus: Core concepts and architectures in deep learning.
- Topics include neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, image and sequence processing.

### Week 10: Introduction to Neural Networks and Deep Learning
- **Lesson 46:** Introduction to Neural Networks and Deep Learning
  - Overview of neural networks, their architecture, and fundamental concepts.
  - Math Focus: Perceptron, activation functions, and forward propagation.

- **Lesson 47:** Activation Functions and Backpropagation
  - Understanding various activation functions and the backpropagation algorithm.
  - Math Focus: Gradient descent, chain rule, and weight updates.

- **Lesson 48:** Training Neural Networks - Optimization and Regularization
  - Techniques for optimizing neural networks and preventing overfitting.
  - Math Focus: Optimization algorithms (SGD, Adam) and regularization methods.

- **Lesson 49:** Introduction to Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
  - Understanding CNN architecture and convolutional operations.
  - Math Focus: Convolution operations, pooling, and feature maps.

- **Lesson 50:** Image Classification with Convolutional Neural Networks
  - Implementing CNNs for image classification tasks.
  - Math Focus: CNN architectures and transfer learning basics.
