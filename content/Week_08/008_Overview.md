# Course Structure

## Module 4: Unsupervised Learning and Dimensionality Reduction (Weeks 7-9)
- Focus: Unsupervised learning techniques and reducing data complexity.
- Topics include clustering, Gaussian Mixture Models, PCA, and t-SNE.

### Week 8: Clustering Techniques
- **Lesson 36:** Introduction to Unsupervised Learning and K-Means Clustering
  - Overview of unsupervised learning and K-Means algorithm.
  - Math Focus: Distance metrics, centroid calculation, and convergence criteria.

- **Lesson 37:** Hierarchical Clustering Methods
  - Understanding agglomerative and divisive clustering approaches.
  - Math Focus: Linkage criteria (single, complete, average) and dendrograms.

- **Lesson 38:** DBSCAN and Density-Based Clustering
  - Implementing DBSCAN for discovering clusters of arbitrary shape.
  - Math Focus: Density reachability and core point definitions.

- **Lesson 39:** Gaussian Mixture Models (GMM)
  - Learning probabilistic clustering with GMM.
  - Math Focus: Expectation-Maximization (EM) algorithm and multivariate Gaussians.

- **Lesson 40:** Clustering Evaluation and Comparison
  - Metrics and methods for evaluating clustering performance.
  - Math Focus: Silhouette score, Davies-Bouldin index, and inertia.

### Week 9: Dimensionality Reduction Techniques
- **Lesson 41:** Introduction to Dimensionality Reduction
  - Understanding the curse of dimensionality and feature extraction.
  - Math Focus: Variance preservation and information retention.

- **Lesson 42:** Principal Component Analysis (PCA)
  - Implementing PCA for linear dimensionality reduction.
  - Math Focus: Eigenvalues, eigenvectors, and covariance matrices.

- **Lesson 43:** Linear Discriminant Analysis (LDA)
  - Using LDA for supervised dimensionality reduction.
  - Math Focus: Between-class and within-class scatter matrices.

- **Lesson 44:** t-SNE for Data Visualization
  - Applying t-SNE for visualizing high-dimensional data.
  - Math Focus: Probability distributions and KL divergence.

- **Lesson 45:** Advanced Dimensionality Reduction Techniques
  - Exploring modern methods including UMAP and autoencoders introduction.
  - Math Focus: Manifold learning, topology preservation, and comparison of techniques.
