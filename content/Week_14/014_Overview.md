  # Course Structure

 ## Module 6: Advanced Machine Learning and Current Trends (Weeks 13-14)
- Focus: Advanced topics and emerging trends in machine learning.
- Topics include reinforcement learning, transfer learning, GANs, attention mechanisms, and transformers.

### Week 14: Advanced Deep Learning Architectures
- **Day 66:** Introduction to Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)
  - Understanding GAN architecture and training dynamics.
  - Math Focus: Minimax game theory and loss functions for generators and discriminators.

- **Day 67:** Transfer Learning and Fine-Tuning
  - Leveraging pre-trained models for new tasks.
  - Math Focus: Feature extraction and domain adaptation principles.

- **Day 68:** Attention Mechanisms and Transformers
  - Exploring attention mechanisms and transformer architecture.
  - Math Focus: Self-attention calculations and transformer architecture.

- **Day 69:** Advanced Transformer Architectures (BERT, GPT)
  - Deep dive into modern transformer-based models.
  - Math Focus: Masked language modeling and autoregressive generation.

- **Day 70:** Applications and Future Trends in AI
  - Real-world applications and emerging trends in machine learning.
  - Math Focus: Scaling laws and efficiency considerations in modern AI systems.
