  # Course Structure

 ## Module 8: Applied AI and Ethical Considerations (Weeks 18-19)
- Focus: Application of AI in various industries and ethical considerations.
- Topics include AI in healthcare, finance, retail, manufacturing, and AI ethics.

### Week 19: AI Applications in Industry and AI Ethics
- **Lesson 91:** AI in Healthcare
  - Overview of AI applications in healthcare and medical diagnosis.
  - Math Focus: Classification metrics, sensitivity, and specificity.

- **Lesson 92:** AI in Finance
  - Understanding AI applications in financial services, fraud detection, and algorithmic trading.
  - Math Focus: Time series analysis and anomaly detection.

- **Lesson 93:** AI in Retail
  - Exploring AI applications in retail, including recommendation systems and demand forecasting.
  - Math Focus: Collaborative filtering and matrix factorization.

- **Lesson 94:** AI in Manufacturing
  - Implementing AI for predictive maintenance, quality control, and process optimization in manufacturing.
  - Math Focus: Anomaly detection, time series forecasting, and classification for defect detection.

- **Lesson 95:** AI Ethics and Responsible AI
  - Understanding ethical considerations in AI development and deployment.
  - Math Focus: Fairness metrics, bias detection, and model interpretability.
