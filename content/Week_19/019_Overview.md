  # Course Structure

 ## Module 8: Applied AI and Ethical Considerations (Weeks 18-19)
- Focus: Application of AI in various industries and ethical considerations.
- Topics: AI in healthcare, finance, retail, manufacturing, AI ethics.

### Week 19: AI in Industry Applications
- **Lesson 91:** Introduction to Applied AI and AI in Healthcare
  - Overview of applied AI across various industries.
  - Deep dive into AI applications in healthcare: diagnosis, treatment planning, drug discovery.
  - Math Focus: Performance metrics for medical AI systems (sensitivity, specificity).

- **Lesson 92:** AI in Finance and Fintech Applications
  - Understanding AI applications in financial services and fintech.
  - Topics: fraud detection, algorithmic trading, credit scoring, risk management.
  - Math Focus: Risk modeling, portfolio optimization, and statistical arbitrage.

- **Lesson 93:** AI in Retail, E-commerce, and Manufacturing
  - Exploring AI applications in retail and manufacturing sectors.
  - Topics: recommendation systems, demand forecasting, quality control, predictive maintenance.
  - Math Focus: Collaborative filtering, time series forecasting methods.

- **Lesson 94:** AI Ethics, Bias, and Fairness
  - Understanding ethical considerations in AI development and deployment.
  - Topics: algorithmic bias, fairness metrics, privacy concerns, transparency.
  - Math Focus: Fairness metrics (demographic parity, equalized odds).

- **Lesson 95:** Responsible AI and Future Trends
  - Best practices for responsible AI development.
  - Topics: AI governance, explainable AI (XAI), regulatory frameworks, future trends.
  - Math Focus: SHAP values, LIME for model interpretability.
