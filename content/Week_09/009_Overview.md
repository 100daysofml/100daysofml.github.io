# Course Structure

## Module 4: Unsupervised Learning and Dimensionality Reduction (Weeks 7-9)
- Focus: Unsupervised learning techniques and reducing data complexity.
- Topics include clustering, Gaussian Mixture Models, PCA, and t-SNE.

### Week 9: Dimensionality Reduction Techniques
- **Lesson 41:** Gaussian Mixture Models (GMM)
  - Understanding probabilistic clustering with GMM.
  - Math Focus: Expectation-Maximization algorithm and probability distributions.

- **Lesson 42:** Introduction to Principal Component Analysis (PCA)
  - Understanding variance-based dimensionality reduction.
  - Math Focus: Eigenvalues, eigenvectors, and covariance matrices.

- **Lesson 43:** Advanced PCA and Applications
  - Implementing PCA for data visualization and preprocessing.
  - Math Focus: Explained variance and component selection.

- **Lesson 44:** t-SNE: t-distributed Stochastic Neighbor Embedding
  - Understanding non-linear dimensionality reduction for visualization.
  - Math Focus: Probability distributions, KL divergence, and gradient descent.

- **Lesson 45:** Comparison of Dimensionality Reduction Techniques
  - Comparing PCA, t-SNE, and other reduction methods.
  - Math Focus: Trade-offs between linear and non-linear methods.
