  # Course Structure

## Module 6: Reinforcement Learning (Week 13)
- Focus: Introduction to reinforcement learning concepts and algorithms.
- Topics include MDPs, Q-Learning, Deep Q-Networks, and policy gradients.

### Week 13: Reinforcement Learning
- **Lesson 61:** Fundamentals of Reinforcement Learning
  - Overview of reinforcement learning concepts and frameworks.
  - Math Focus: Reward function optimization and decision-making processes.

- **Lesson 62:** Markov Decision Processes in Reinforcement Learning
  - Understanding MDPs and their role in reinforcement learning.
  - Math Focus: Transition probability matrices and state-value functions.

- **Lesson 63:** Basics of Q-Learning
  - Learning and implementing the Q-learning algorithm.
  - Math Focus: Bellman equation and value estimation.

- **Lesson 64:** Deep Q-Networks (DQN) and Their Applications
  - Implementing DQNs in complex environments.
  - Math Focus: Neural networks integration with Q-learning and loss functions.

- **Lesson 65:** Policy Gradient Methods in Reinforcement Learning
  - Understanding policy gradient methods and their implementation.
  - Math Focus: Policy optimization techniques and gradient ascent.
