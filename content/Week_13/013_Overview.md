# Course Structure

## Module 6: Advanced Machine Learning and Current Trends (Weeks 13-14)
- Focus: Advanced topics and emerging trends in machine learning.
- Topics include reinforcement learning, transfer learning, GANs, and attention mechanisms.

### Week 13: Advanced Machine Learning Techniques

- **Lesson 61:** Introduction to Reinforcement Learning
  - Overview of reinforcement learning paradigm and key concepts.
  - Math Focus: Markov Decision Processes, rewards, and value functions.

- **Lesson 62:** Q-Learning and Deep Q-Networks
  - Understanding Q-Learning algorithm and Deep Q-Networks (DQN).
  - Math Focus: Q-value updates and temporal difference learning.

- **Lesson 63:** Transfer Learning
  - Understanding transfer learning and its applications in deep learning.
  - Math Focus: Feature extraction, fine-tuning, and domain adaptation.

- **Lesson 64:** Introduction to Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)
  - Learning about GANs architecture and training process.
  - Math Focus: Generator and discriminator loss functions, Nash equilibrium.

- **Lesson 65:** Attention Mechanisms and Transformers Overview
  - Exploring attention mechanisms and introduction to transformer architecture.
  - Math Focus: Self-attention, multi-head attention, and positional encoding.
